How a fresh Burlington permit becomes your next job

A building permit is the one public document that proves a specific homeowner at a specific address has committed money to a specific project — this week, not someday. Recent filings on Colburn St, Edgemont Ave, Macon Rd and Lucy Rd each represent an owner who has already decided to build. For contractors, that changes the sales conversation entirely.

Here is the practical play:

  • Follow the demolition permits. With 23 recent demo filings, Burlington has a real pipeline of properties about to need framing, mechanical, electrical and finish trades. The demo permit tells you where, before the rebuild goes out to bid.
