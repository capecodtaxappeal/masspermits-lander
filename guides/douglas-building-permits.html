Douglas, MA Building Permits: Where the Work Is Right Now

What Douglas permit filings say about the local market

MassPermits is currently tracking 129 recent building permits in Douglas, and the trade mix tells a clear story: this is a mechanical-trades town. General building permits lead at 32, but they barely edge out HVAC at 29 — a gap of just three filings. Add gas (21) and plumbing (19) and the mechanical trades together account for 69 permits, more than double the building category on its own. Electrical sits at 25, and demolition is the smallest slice at 3.

That ratio matters if you're hunting contractor jobs in Douglas, MA. When HVAC, gas, and plumbing filings nearly keep pace with — and combined, far outnumber — general building work, homeowners here are investing in the systems inside their houses: heating and cooling swaps, gas line work, water heaters, and the electrical upgrades that ride along with them. With only 3 demolition permits on the board, this is not a tear-down-and-rebuild market. It's an upgrade market.
