Fitchburg Building Permits: Where the Work Is Right Now

We're currently tracking 149 recent building permits in Fitchburg, and the mix tells a clear story: this is a mechanical-systems town. HVAC is the single busiest trade with 38 permits — well ahead of every other category — and when you add gas (20) and plumbing (19), mechanical work accounts for more than half of everything filed. In a city full of older triple-deckers, mill-era buildings, and postwar housing stock, that's exactly what you'd expect: owners aren't building new so much as replacing boilers, converting heating systems, and upgrading the guts of buildings that have been standing for a century.

Septic, at just 6 permits, confirms the flip side: most of the city is on municipal sewer, so this is not a septic market the way outlying towns are.

For HVAC contractors especially, the math in Fitchburg is compelling.
