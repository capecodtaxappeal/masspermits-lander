Grafton Building Permits: What 136 Recent Filings Say About Where the Work Is

If you run a mechanical shop in Central Mass, Grafton is quietly one of the busiest boards you can watch.

The Grafton trade mix, by the numbers

  • Gas — 25 permits. Tied for the largest single category. Gas piping filings travel with boiler swaps, range conversions, generator hookups and heating upgrades.
  • Electrical — 25 permits. Tied with gas at the top. Panel upgrades, EV charger circuits and service work all live here.
  • Plumbing — 24 permits and HVAC — 24 permits. A dead heat, one filing behind the leaders — which tells you these projects are moving together as whole-system jobs.
  • Building — 18 permits. The structural work: additions, remodels, decks and repairs.

  • Fire/Sprinkler — 9 permits. The smallest category on the board, but steady — and often tied to larger commercial or multi-unit projects worth tracking.
