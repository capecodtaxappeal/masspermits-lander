How a fresh permit becomes your next job

Every permit in that stack of 349 is a homeowner or property owner who has already decided to spend money. That is what makes permit data the highest-intent lead source in construction. A filing tells you the address, the project type and the timing — and a project that just pulled one trade permit usually needs others. The homeowner on West Meadow Rd who pulled a plumbing permit may need an electrician next month. The demo filing on Ayer St is a general contractor's build opportunity a season from now.

Why speed wins in this market

With plumbing and gas dominating Haverhill's filings, mechanical contractors face the most competition — but also the most volume. Renovation permits are the richest cross-sell: a remodel on Standish Rd or Cogswell St touches plumbing, electrical and carpentry all at once, and the owner rarely has every trade lined up on day one.
