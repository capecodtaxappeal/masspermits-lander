How a fresh Norton permit becomes your next job

A building permit is the earliest public signal that money is moving on a property. When an owner on Brookwood Ln or Avery Rd files for an addition, they have already cleared the hardest hurdles: budget, decision, and commitment. What they usually have not done is line up every trade the project needs.

That is the window. The general contractor may be set, but the sprinkler sub, the HVAC changeout, the electrical service upgrade, or the follow-on landscaping and paving often are not. Reaching an owner in the days after they file — when the project is real but the sub list is open — beats cold-calling a neighborhood by months. You are not asking "are you planning any work?"
