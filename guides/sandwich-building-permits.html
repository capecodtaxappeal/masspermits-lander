That is a 17-to-1 ratio in favor of working on houses that already exist. Sandwich is the oldest town on Cape Cod, and its permit file reads exactly like it: owners of established homes on streets like Shawme Road, Dexter Avenue and Town Neck Road are opening up kitchens, finishing rooms and updating aging systems rather than breaking ground on new lots.
